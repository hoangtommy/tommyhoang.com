About

My family immigrated to the states when I was 4. I grew up playing 2-hand touch football on the cul-de-sac with other latchkey kids. I attended a high school where 80% of the students were Vietnamese and most of us had free & reduced lunch. It was in drama class that I'd meet the love of my life. I've done a lot of things for a living (education, real estate, tech), but feel most alive when I'm snowboarding and eating good food with my high school sweetheart.
